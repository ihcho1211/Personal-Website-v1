About

At HumanX Lab, I lead the biology side of biosensor development. I am building a batch-production, end-to-end biological pipeline using hiPSCs and other cell lines.

I am building biosensor platforms with high quality, consistency, and reproducibility, with the goal of interfacing them with bio-silicon sensing systems. My current focus is on the biological side of the platform.

My background combines neuroscience, cell biology, advanced imaging, molecular biology, CRISPR-based methods, and upstream bioprocessing. Before joining HumanX Lab, I worked at Adimab and previously held research roles at Dartmouth studying synaptic transmission, neuronal excitability, and ion channel regulation.
